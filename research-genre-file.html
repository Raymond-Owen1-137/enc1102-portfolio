What Is Research Genre Production?

Producing and reflecting on different academic genres—such as literature reviews and research papers—and the choices and constraints of each.

Artifacts

  • Literature Review
  • Final Research Paper

Reflection

Through my Literature Review and Final Research Paper, I demonstrate Research Genre Production by navigating distinct academic conventions. In the Literature Review, I synthesized existing scholarship with structured headings, thematic coding, and visual summaries. In the Final Paper, I combined quantitative sentiment analysis with close rhetorical readings, following conventions for data presentation, chart annotation, and formal academic prose. Each genre required adapting tone, structure, and methodological framing to meet audience expectations—reviewers of scholarly literature versus readers of a data-driven rhetorical study. These artifacts reflect my skill in choosing appropriate strategies, negotiating assignment constraints, and producing polished, audience-centered research genres.
